Hand Baggage

  • Checked Baggage
    • 2 Pc. / 23 Kgs. each (Check airlines for more information)
    • Combined dimensions of 2 pieces (length * width * height) shouldn't exceed 107 inches/273cm, provided neither piece exceeds 62 inches/ 158cm
  • Carry on Baggage
    • 1 Pc. / 8 Kgs. (Check airlines for more information)
    • Shouldn't exceed 8 inches/20cm x 16 inches/40cm x 22 inches/55cm and 18 lbs/8 kg, plus one personal item.
