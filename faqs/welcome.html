BACK WELCOME TO USA

This page contains information regarding all the basic things that need to be looked into when a student arrives at Santa Clara University. This information will help you in your initial settling in period. By putting up the information we do not advocate any specific brand or company. This information is put up purely going by the past experience of Indian Students at SCU by the sole intent of making the transition of new students much smoother.


Bank Account

  • Most Indian students here at SCU have an account with Bank of America or Chase Bank. The Bank of America and Chase Bank offers student accounts. You would be able to find out more information regarding this by visiting one of the branches.
  • Bank of America is right opposite to the main SCU entrance, across the road. It should be about 5 minutes walk from the main SCU entrance gate.
  • Chase Bank is 15 minutes by VTA (Bus)
  • Going by the past experience of Indian students at SCU, opening an account is a very easy procedure. The documents required to open an account just include your I-20 and passport.
  • However, other banks like CitiBank, and Wells Fargo are there as well that offer accounts for students. You are most welcome to explore other options!

Car Sharing

  • Zipcar is a car sharing facility used by various Indian students enrolled in SCU.
  • Students rent out the car on an hourly basis. You are required to become a member in order to use this facility.
  • You will initially need to add you Indian driving license (written in English) to become a member.
  • When you call zipcar enquire about any ongoing offers for SCU students, you are most likely to get lucky to get discounts!
  • You can explore this option in detail by visiting the website!

Public Transport

  • Santa Clara Caltrain Station is right opposite SCU main entrance. You can travel to places like San Francisco, etc in $8!
  • Santa Clara Transit Center is the main VTA Bus stop to travel other places. Other VTA stops are around the university itself.
  • VTA Bus costs $2.5 for 2 hours. You can make a physical card (Clipper card) or throug Apple phone's digital wallet by visiting the website!

Social Security Number(SSN)

  • All the students who would be working on-campus or off-campus for internships/full-time job would need a Social Security card. The pre-requisite to generate a SSN would a letter of employment/letter of intent to hire from the employer.
  • You need to schedule an appointment at SSN office (closest one available is San Jose Downtown) and get your verifications done in-person.
  • Once, it's verified, you will recieve the SSN in 7-10 business days via mail and then, follow procesdures provided by university to complete onboarding.
